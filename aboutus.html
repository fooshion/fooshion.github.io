About Fooshion Hello!

Fooshion, a fusion of food and fashion, is a webapp created by 5 Girls Who Code students at the AOL Charitable Fund @ CUNY Research Center. Fooshion is perfect for you if you ever find yourself wondering what to wear or what to eat to/for certain events. Users can upload their recipes and outfits and choose an event, so other users can go through and gather fashion and food ideas. Our goal is simple; we want people to have somewhere to look when they want real ideas from real people.
